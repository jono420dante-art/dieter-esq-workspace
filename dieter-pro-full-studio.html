How it works
  • Lyrics — write your own, or Generate Lyrics (starter draft) / Optimize (clean & tighten).

  • Vocal gender — Female or Male (used when lyrics are present).
  • Style — mood + instruments (e.g. Grand Piano, Melodic Trap, Post Rock); combined with lyrics for the final sound.
  • Then Create for melody + vocals (or instrumental).

♫ Digital distributors (Spotify, Apple, YouTube Music, Napster, …)
Streaming platforms do not accept uploads directly from artists. You release through a distributor; they deliver to Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon, YouTube Music, Napster, TikTok, and more.
DistroKidFlat annual fee, fast delivery to all major stores. TuneCorePer-release pricing; strong reporting tools. CD BabyOne-time fee per release + optional pro services. LANDRDistribution bundled with mastering tools. AmuseFree tier available; mobile-first workflow. RouteNoteFree distribution option with revenue share.
