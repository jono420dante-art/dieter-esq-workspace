# Free vocal samples (external sites)

Use these for **drag-and-drop** into Beat Lab, DIETER-PRO, or any DAW. Always read each site’s **license** before publishing or monetizing.

## Sources

| Source | URL | Notes |
|--------|-----|--------|
| **LANDR** (free vocal collections) | [samples.landr.com/collections/free-vocal-samples](https://samples.landr.com/collections/free-vocal-samples) | Curated free vocal collections |
| **Slooply** | [slooply.com/samples/instrument/vocal](https://slooply.com/samples/instrument/vocal) | Filter: vocal — check “100% free” / license per pack |
| **Output** | [output.com/samples/vocal-samples](https://output.com/samples/vocal-samples) | Free packs (account may be required) |
| **Loopazon** | [loopazon.com](https://loopazon.com) | Royalty-free listings — verify license per download |

## Workflow

1. **Download** packs (ZIP or installer).  
2. **Unzip** into a folder you keep for samples (e.g. `~/Music/DIETER Samples/vocals`).  
3. **Drag** WAV/MP3 into your studio: Beat Lab upload, sampler, or timeline.
